DEFEND POP PUNK wars twice a week on Tuesdays and Fridays at 6:00pm CDT. Participation in optional but encouraged. All we ask is that you make an honest effort and have fun.

RULES
There's only two hard rules that will result in being kicked from the clan.

1) Must use both attacks in the war (no exceptions)
2) No attacking solely for the War Loot Bonus (no exceptions)

If you break these rules you find yourself kicked. Worry not, we will give you a second chance if asked. (You must wait for the 24-hr cooldown before you can request back in)

GUIDELINES

  • Do not worry about contributing to the defensive Clan Castles. Arrangements have already been made to ensure they get filled.
  • Try to attack the highest base that you are comfortable 3-starring.
  • If all of the bases that you are comfortable 3-starring are attacked, make a practice attack on a defeated base. Attacking for loot is not allowed.
  • Ask for questions or advice if you want an opinion before your war attack.
  • Don't forget to request for troops before you attack.
  • Check out our resources page for clan war tips and videos!
